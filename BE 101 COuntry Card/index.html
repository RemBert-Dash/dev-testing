Surprising facts

  • No Human Majority: It is often said that only 5% of New Zealand's population is human, with the vast majority of inhabitants being animals.
  • Penguin Capital: New Zealand has more species of penguin than any other country.
  • Steepest Street: Dunedin boasts Baldwin Street, formerly recognized as the steepest street in the world.

Famous people

  • Lorde: The internationally acclaimed singer-songwriter known for hits like "Royals" and "Green Light."
  • Peter Jackson: The renowned filmmaker behind the "Lord of the Rings" and "Hobbit" trilogies.
  • Edmund Hillary: The legendary mountaineer who, along with Tenzing Norgay, was the first to reach the summit of Mount Everest.
